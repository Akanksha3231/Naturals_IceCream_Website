About Us

Naturals

Naturals ice cream truly justifies its name, the ice cream flavours you would find here are super unique and truly natural. ranging from the common chocolate, mango, vanilla, strawberry to unique Anjeer, roasted almond, tender coconut, Sitaphal, Malai ice cream etc. The ice cream here doesn't only have unique flavours but the texture of ice cream is creamy and tastes super milky.


The best part about the ice creams served here are that we are not synthetically flavoured but the chunks of whatever ice cream you choose can be tasted and felt in your mouth. Pricing is decent and well, it is justified for the quantity they serve.
